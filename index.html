Navigating Admission into Ambrose Alli University (AAU)

Enrolling in Ambrose Alli University (AAU) can be an exciting step toward your academic journey. What sets AAU apart is the absence of a post UTME exam requirement, provided you've successfully completed your JAMB or UTME exams. This relief means you can avoid the additional stress of another examination. However, you must meet certain prerequisites, including your secondary school external results such as WAEC or NECO.

To get started on your AAU enrollment journey, follow these essential steps:

  1. Meet the Course Minimum Score: Ensure you've achieved the minimum score required for the course you wish to study through your JAMB result.
  2. Visit a Reliable Cyber Cafe: Once AAU's online registration has commenced, seek out a reputable and trustworthy cyber cafe. It's crucial to work with professionals who stay updated on any changes in the registration process. They are motivated to stay informed as their business relies on it. Avoid attempting the registration on your own, or entrusting it to someone inexperienced.

Here are the documents you'll need for the online registration:

  • Affidavit of age declaration or original birth certificate.
  • Affidavit of local government of origin.
  • A copy of your SSCE results (either WAEC, NECO, or both).
  • A recent passport photograph.
  • SSCE scratch cards for one result, and two if you're using two results for registration.
  • A copy of Original UTME result printout.

AAU has its quirks, and it may take some time before you receive your admission letter. Once you do, make sure to print it out. When it comes to your acceptance fee, it's advisable to pay as soon as possible. We can't emphasize this enough: entrust this task to a trustworthy cyber cafe and collect a receipt for your records. Errors can occur, and given the significance of acceptance and tuition fees, it's not an area where you want to take any chances. Never attempt it yourself.Be cautious about allowing anyone other than your legitimate school supporters to make payments on your behalf, as scams abound.

AAU's admission process can be a bit unique, but with the right approach and attention to detail, you can navigate it successfully.
